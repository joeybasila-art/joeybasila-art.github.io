About my journey

Welcome to this sacred space where I translate the ancient language of the stars. I have long been drawn to the esoteric and astrological realms. What began as a personal journey of self study eventually evolved into a professional path rooted in devotion, curiosity, and a desire to help others reconnect with themselves through evolutionary astrology. My approach is through weaving practical understanding, intuitive insight, and lived experience into each reading. My knowledge of astrology was further refined through the mentorship of two beautiful women whose integrity, wisdom, and humanity has shaped the way I practice and share this work today.

Whether you are navigating a transition, seeking clarity, or answering a call toward deeper self awareness, I am here to help bridge the wisdom woven in your chart into grounded and practical understanding.

A Synastry Reading is an in depth comparison that overlays two Birth Charts that will explore: Relational and emotional compatibility, communication dynamics, attraction and chemistry, conflict patterns, and growth areas within the connection, through the lens of Evolutionary Astrology.

Transit Reading

A Transit Reading is the relationship between the current planetary movements (transits) in the sky and the exact position of the planetary bodies in your natal Birth chart. This acts as a weather report for how the transits are moving across your natal chart throughout the year. These movements help you understand certain triggers, events, lessons and opportunities for you to understand and navigate with more clarity.
